Demonstration of X-Rec

Experimental Platform

For the underlying servers, the X-server, the TP and the TTP are implemented with Apache Tomcat and deployed on a Supermicro dual-socket server with 2 Processors Intel(R) Xeon(R) 10-core CPU E5-2680 v2 at 2.80GHz (with hyperthreading, we have 40 virtual cores in all). For X-client, we use a laptop with Intel Core i7 2.6GHz processor.

Dataset

For the demonstration, we used sampled real-world traces from Movielens.
